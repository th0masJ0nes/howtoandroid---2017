phone_androidtablet_androidPhones & Tablets
Split Screen:

To activate split screen on your device, hold down the app tray buttion for a seconds and select the app you want to share your screen with.

Quick Switch:

To use quick switch between your apps, double tap the app tray button to switch between them.

Direct Reply:

When you get a message, a notification will show at the top of your screen. swipe from the top of your screen to see the notification.

Bundled notifications:

If an app has lots of notifications these will be bundled into a small dropdown card will each one displayed. You can more then one bundled notification.

Notification Control:

Control how your phone reacts to each notification for each app, allow some to override "do not disturb" so they still make a sound when activated. you can also do a lot more into how your device reacts on what it does when you get a new notification.

Display Size:

You can change the size of your display size in settings, this can be font size, icon size, wallpaper, brightness and lots more to make your dispaly to how you want it.
